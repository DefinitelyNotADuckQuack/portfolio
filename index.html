About Me

Hi! I'm a 25-year-old freelancer with a strong foundation in programming and a specialization in art, illustration, and 2D animation. My work sits at the intersection of creativity and technology—I bring ideas to life not just through visuals, but also through code.

As a Python full-stack developer, I build responsive, visually engaging websites and applications. I enjoy combining the precision of programming with the expressive nature of design, creating solutions that are both functional and aesthetically refined. My background in the arts gives me a unique perspective in tech projects, especially when it comes to UI/UX and visual storytelling. Whether I'm animating a character or structuring an app, I care deeply about detail, clarity, and the user experience. I also love supporting other creatives and believe that collaboration makes everything better.
